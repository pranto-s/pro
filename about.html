Fahad is the founding Product Lead at Pathao (the most popular Bangladeshi super-app) when he was just 17 years old, and yet this wasn't even his first entrepreneurial experience. About 8 years later, he is now leading the company as Senior Vice President & Head of Product.

He led and launched several highly successful products across ride sharing, food delivery, payments, buy-now-pay-later, etc. which shaped how people use digital services in Bangladesh and Nepal.

Pathao has 8 million users, 300,000 drivers and delivery agents, 30,000 merchants, and 10,000 restaurants on their network. They are operating in 5 cities across Bangladesh and Nepal.

Fahad’s business podcast/channel has garnered 1 million+ views across YouTube, Facebook and other platforms.

He studied finance at IBA, University of Dhaka.
